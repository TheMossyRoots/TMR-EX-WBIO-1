## What This Is

This is a real product experiment run by The Mossy Roots.

You are contributing to the construction of a real product called 
**DubIndia** — a platform that enables YouTube creators to dub their 
videos into Indian languages using AI.

But the product is not the only thing being built here.

This experiment is also testing whether real project work, under strict 
ownership and documentation, can generate a stronger signal of 
professional capability than a traditional resume.

Your artifacts and your documented decisions are proof of what you can 
actually do. That proof becomes your whobuilt.io profile.

---

## The Product: DubIndia

**Problem:** YouTube creators cannot reach India's non-English speaking 
audience — 900 million people — because dubbing is expensive and slow.

**Solution:** A platform that accepts a YouTube URL, extracts the audio, 
transcribes it, translates it via Sarvam AI into major Indian languages, 
and outputs a dubbed video ready to publish.

**Target User:** Indian and global YouTube creators with 10K–500K 
subscribers who want to grow their Indian audience.

**Technology:** Sarvam AI for speech and translation. Stack decisions 
are made by the CTO in Phase 2.

---

## How The Experiment Works

This project follows a strict waterfall sequence:
```
Founder → PM → CTO → Developer → Designer → Growth
```

Each role:
- Owns one phase completely
- Has no live collaboration with other contributors
- Receives no guidance from the operator during their streaks
- Must deliver artifacts and a Decision Rationale at the end of each streak
- Must hand off cleanly so the next person can start without confusion

**You will not be mentored. You will not be micromanaged. You own your 
phase entirely.**

---

## Your Commitment

Each contributor completes a maximum of 2 streaks.

You choose your streak length upfront — 3 days or 7 days — and declare 
exactly what you will deliver before you start.

At the end of each streak you submit two things:

1. **Your Artifact** — the actual deliverable for your role
2. **Your Decision Rationale** — a structured document explaining every 
decision you made, what alternatives you considered, and why you chose 
what you chose

Both go into your folder in this repository. Nothing else is accepted.

---

## The Rules

1. You only write to your own folder. You can read everything else.
2. You do not modify previous phases. Ever.
3. You do not ask the operator for guidance during your streak.
4. If you are blocked, you document the blocker and work around it.
5. If you cannot finish your streak, you notify the operator immediately 
   and update your documentation before you leave.
6. Your Decision Rationale is mandatory. An artifact without it is an 
   incomplete submission.

## What You Get

- A verified proof-of-work profile on whobuilt.io

- A timestamped GitHub record of everything you built and decided
- Professional connections with every contributor in the chain

This is unpaid. The return is proof — real, verifiable, documented proof
